Tally Counter is a simple counting app. Tap to count. Multiple counters, haptic feedback, and an Apple Watch companion app included.

  • Apple Watch support
  • Home Screen widget
  • Haptic feedback

FAQ

How do I reset a counter?
Swipe left on a counter or use the reset button.

Does it sync between devices?
Counters sync between iPhone and Apple Watch.

How do I remove ads?
Go to Settings and tap "Remove Ads" to make a one-time purchase.
